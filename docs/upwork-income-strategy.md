# Upwork Income Strategy

Persistent reference for income planning, phase progression, and
progress tracking. Load in every Upwork session. Re-run analysis
periodically — do not treat rate figures or timelines as fixed.
Last full analysis: 2026-04-17.

---

## Context and constraints

- Timeframe: 12-24 months, concurrent with MFT grad school + clinical
  internship (licensed therapist 2027)
- Available time: ~25-30 hours/week total (billable + non-billable)
  during school; may shift as schedule changes
- Income goal: $4k+/month, ideally stable within ~6 months
- Urgency: real earnings needed to begin within ~8 weeks from 2026-04-17
- Strategic purpose: smooth the grad school to licensure to agency/
  private practice transition financially

---

## Income math (reference — re-run periodically)

Assume ~60% billable ratio (beginners run 50-60%; experienced higher).

| Total hrs/week | Billable hrs/week | Billable hrs/month | Rate needed for $4k |
|----------------|-------------------|-------------------|---------------------|
| 30             | 18                | 72                | ~$55/hr effective   |
| 25             | 15                | 60                | ~$67/hr effective   |
| 20             | 12                | 48                | ~$83/hr effective   |

At 30 hrs/week, $4k/month is achievable at $55-60/hr effective —
well within reach once past the early-discount phase.

At 25 hrs/week, requires staying at or above current public rate
with minimal discounting.

At 20 hrs/week, requires specialist niche pricing — medium-term
outcome, not near-term.

## Phase structure

### Phase 1 — Golden Goose (current)
**Goal:** First 5-15 reviews, 100% JSS, credibility flywheel.
**Income expectation:** Below target — this is investment phase.
**Time horizon:** 0-3 months from first contract.
**Tactics:**
- Fixed-price or hourly, clearly scoped, fast-close jobs
- Optimize for: quick hire, fast delivery, substantive written review
- Rate is secondary — slightly under-price to win traction
- Keep public profile rate at $65/hr as anchor
- Target 20-30 proposals/week; treat proposal writing as core work
- Over-communicate, deliver Loom walkthroughs, prompt close + review

**Exit criteria:** 5+ reviews, 100% JSS, at least 1 repeat client

### Phase 2 — Niche Experiment (next)
**Goal:** Identify highest-value lane; push toward $2k-$3k/month.
**Time horizon:** Months 2-4.
**Tactics:**
- Run 60-90 day niche experiment: tilt 60-70% of proposals toward
  2-3 candidate niches
- Track per niche: reply rate, close rate, contract size, speed,
  ease of delivery, client quality
- Candidate niches: healthcare ops automation, health-adjacent
  coaching/wellness ops, safety-conscious AI workflow design
- Begin accepting longer hourly/weekly anchor contracts in winning niche
- Update profile + portfolio with each win (outcomes + metrics)

**Exit criteria:** Clear niche front-runner; $2k-$3k in a single month

### Phase 3 — Niche Specialist (target state)
**Goal:** Stable $4k+/month at ~30 hrs/week.
**Time horizon:** Months 3-6.
**Tactics:**
- Niche profile headline and overview around winning vertical
- Client mix: 1-2 anchor clients (50-70% of monthly target) +
  2-4 fixed-price sprints/month
- Productized offers: named outcomes, fixed prices, defined scope
- Raise public rate to $80-90/hr at ~5-7 reviews; $100-125/hr
  at 15+ reviews + $40k earned
- Push fixed-price minimums up as profile strengthens

**Exit criteria:** Stable month at $4k+; 2+ anchor clients

## Rate ladder (re-evaluate at each milestone)

| Milestone | Public rate | Actual effective rate |
|-----------|-------------|----------------------|
| Now (0 reviews) | $65/hr | $45-65/hr, discounts OK in Phase 1 |
| 5-7 reviews | Raise to $80-90/hr | $70-100/hr |
| 15+ reviews, $40k earned | Raise to $100-125/hr | $100-200/hr |

These are orientation figures from 2026-04-17 analysis.
Re-run before each rate change decision.

---

## Client mix model

**Phase 1:** Many small contracts. Volume over revenue.

**Phase 2-3 target mix:**
- 1-2 anchor clients: hourly or retainer, 8-12 hrs/week each,
  covers 50-70% of monthly income target
- 2-4 fixed-price sprints/month: $500-$1,500 each, niche-aligned,
  fast scope + deliver + close cycle
