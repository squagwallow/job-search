## Overview / summary
I design, document, and oversee AI-powered workflows for health, service, and professional organizations. My focus is taking a messy operational problem, shaping it into a clear workflow, and then building a no-code + LLM system that your team can actually run and understand.

One personal project I am particularly proud of is called Cornflower Health — an end-to-end health data pipeline I've been running and iterating on for over seven months for my own wearable data. It pulls Apple Watch data into a webhook backend, normalizes it into a structured schema, syncs it into Notion dashboards, and layers LLM-based coaching on top. Some parts are polished, some are still evolving, but every architecture choice, debugging session, and trade-off is documented. That project is less a "finished product" and more a live lab: I've broken and rebuilt pieces of the pipeline several times, experimented with prompts and data models, and developed a deep feel for where LLMs fail in real health-related workflows. That hands-on experimentation is what I bring into client work.

What I bring to your team:
- I can turn a vague idea ("we're copy-pasting data between tools") into a scoped, observable workflow and a concrete system design.
- I can direct AI tools as a development team — prompting, chaining, and orchestrating them across multi-session builds while maintaining continuity.
- I understand clinical and safety risk: I've written a clinical AI failure-mode case study describing how temporal-context errors in LLMs produce misleading or clinically unsafe outputs. I can write for both clinical and engineering stakeholders, and I know the difference in what each group needs to see.

My background and domain focus:
I'm currently completing an MFT clinical internship (I will be a licensed therapist in 2027), and I have prior experience in healthcare operations, public health writing, and workshop facilitation. That means I'm comfortable talking about symptoms, safety plans, and care teams on one side — and data models, pipelines, and workflows on the other.

What I'm offering on Upwork right now:

AI Workflow Audit & Brief (Fixed price $400)
A focused engagement where I review your current process, map the workflow, identify automation and AI opportunities, and deliver a visual brief you can hand to an internal team or another implementer. I use the same visual system I built for my case studies, with clear diagrams, screenshots, and a narrative that non-technical stakeholders can follow.

End-to-end AI workflow design and implementation:
For teams in healthcare, behavioral health, coaching, or operations who want to go beyond "ChatGPT in a tab." This includes discovery, architecture diagrams, no-code build (e.g., Notion, automation tools, LLM APIs), iterative testing, and handoff docs.
