# LinkedIn Profile

**[STUB — paste your LinkedIn profile content. See POPULATED BY at the end.]**

## What belongs here

Full text of your current LinkedIn profile:

- **Headline**
- **About section** (full long-form text)
- **Experience** — each role with title, company, dates, location, description
- **Education**
- **Licenses & certifications**
- **Skills** (top skills list)
- **Featured / projects** — if any
- **Location**
- **Open to work** status (and what you've specified)

Goal: one-shot. The LLM loads this file and has your complete LinkedIn-facing identity without scraping the live profile.
