# General-Jobs Strategy

**[STUB — populate with your own content. See POPULATED BY at the end.]**

## What belongs here

### Availability
Part-time vs. full-time, hours per week, start date, notice period, any schedule constraints.

### Target pay
Annual range, hourly range for contract, minimum acceptable.

### Target role types
What kinds of roles you're applying for in the non-Upwork channel. Name them specifically.

### Location / remote posture
Remote-only, hybrid OK with limits, willing to relocate, etc.

### Hard dealbreakers (used by the job search prompt)
Examples:
- PhD required → skip
- On-site only → skip
- Specific industries to avoid → skip
- Salary below $X → skip
- Security clearance required → skip

### Signals of a promising listing
What makes you upgrade to `high` priority.

### Signals of a weak listing
Short of a hard dealbreaker but reason to downgrade or skip.
